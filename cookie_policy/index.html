1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website or use an app. They help websites remember your actions and preferences over time. Cookies can be “session cookies” that expire when you close your browser, or “persistent cookies” that remain on your device for a set period.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to support the core functionality of our website and App, improve user experience, and for analytics and marketing purposes. Specifically, we use cookies for:

A. Essential / Functional Cookies

  • Remembering login sessions so you stay signed in while navigating the site or app.
  • Maintaining preferences and settings, such as language or display options.
  • Enabling secure transactions and preventing fraudulent activity.

B. Analytics & Performance Cookies

  • Tracking usage patterns such as which pages are visited most frequently and how users navigate through the site.
  • Measuring performance of features and content to improve functionality.
  • Recording crash reports, errors, and load times to ensure smooth operation of the App and website.

C. Personalization Cookies

  • Remembering your user preferences to personalize the content displayed, such as recent projects, favorite sections, or suggested features.
  • Providing a more tailored experience by remembering your choices and interactions.

D. Security Cookies

  • Verifying your identity when logging in or performing sensitive actions.
  • Protecting against malicious activity, such as cross-site scripting or unauthorized access.
  • Ensuring the integrity of sessions and account security.

E. Marketing & Advertising Cookies

  • Delivering advertisements that are relevant to your interests.
  • Measuring the effectiveness of ad campaigns and promotions.
